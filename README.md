# Abu Dhabi Hotel Performance Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard analyzing hotel performance across Abu Dhabi zones (2019–2024)

---
## 📌 Project Overview
This dashboard provides a clear view of hotel performance across Abu Dhabi from 2019 to 2024.  
It highlights how pricing, demand, and revenue vary across zones and over time through:

- Zone-level performance analysis  
- ADR and RevPAR trends  
- Occupancy patterns across quarters  
- Revenue changes across years  
- Interactive slicers for Year, Quarter, and Zone  

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation
Data was cleaned and transformed in Power Query: 
- Removed duplicates
- Fixed data types 
- Add date column
- Added a **Date Table** (Year, Quarter, Month)  
- Renamed columns for clarity  
- Filtered rows to show only abu dhabi zones
- Removed unused columns 

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
- **YoY Growth %**  
- **Occupancy %**  
- **RevPAR (AED)**  
- **ADR (AED)**  
- **Total Revenue (AED)**  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- **Q1 and Q4 are the strongest quarters**, showing peak hotel performance.  
- **ADR increased over time**, reflecting stronger pricing power in recent years.  
- **2020 shows the lowest revenue**, due to the impact of COVID‑19.  
- **Saadiyat is the most profitable zone**, with the highest ADR and strong RevPAR.  
- After COVID, **the market shows clear recovery**, with improvements in both ADR and occupancy.  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- Five KPI cards showing YoY Growth %, Occupancy %, RevPAR, ADR, and Total Revenue  
- Zone-level comparison visuals for revenue and RevPAR  
- Year and Quarter trend analysis for revenue, ADR, and occupancy  
- Interactive slicers for Year, Quarter, and Zone  
- Clean, business-focused layout designed for quick decision-making
